Welcome to Fit Friends

A fun way to exercise and build healthy habits.

Why Fit Friends?


We want to make exercise a social activity and provide an aspect of competition to encourage people to get active. Fit Friends provides fun ways to get active on your own or with your friends. Our goal is to help you achieve your goals, whether you are by yourself, with some friends, or on a team.


What can you do with Fit Friends?




Set personal goals and earn badges for your achievements.


Set goals with a group of friends, and compete to see who can be the most active.


Compete with a team of friends against other teams in a fitness challenge.
